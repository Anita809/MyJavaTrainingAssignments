Below is a list of documents required to apply for a driving license :

  1. Recent Passport Size Photograph of the applicant– 3 Copies

  2. Residential Address Proof ( Aadhar card, Ration Card, Voter ID, Electricity bill)

  3. Valid Age Proof Documents ( Passport, Aadhar card, Birth Certificate, School living certificate)

  4. Minimum Age Group of 18 Years and above to drive vehicles with a gear

  5. Minimum Age Group of 16 Years to drive vehicles without gear
